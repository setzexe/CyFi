What is CyFi?

Purpose CyFi is a personal finance tracker built for simplicity and security. It lets you manually manage accounts, transactions, and recurring bills from one unified dashboard.

Why Built CyFi was built due to many modern systems being complex and utilizing data that is not needed. Rather than connecting to a bank, you are in control of your own data. No real data is at risk.
